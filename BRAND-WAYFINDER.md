# Wayfinder Brand Direction

## Status

The client selected **The Wayfinder**. This decision supersedes earlier recommendations for Open Horizon and the Safari Window.

The existing concept board is a visual direction, not production artwork. Redraw it cleanly before using it as the final website logo.

## Brand Idea

Wayfinder positions Holiday Kenya Safaris as the confident local guide that turns the desire to travel into a clear, well-managed journey.

It should communicate:

- Competent planning.
- Trustworthy guidance.
- Forward movement.
- Local expertise.
- Premium quality without distance or intimidation.

It must not communicate:

- A navigation app.
- A software platform.
- A car dealership.
- A formal corporate procurement portal.
- A cheap package-poster business.

## Identity Palette

| Role | Name | HEX | Intended use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | Midnight Navy | `#182B3A` | Wordmark, headings, footer, high-trust surfaces |
| Secondary | Lake Teal | `#2C7A78` | Links, active states, secondary actions, graphic details |
| Action accent | Saffron | `#E1A62B` | Primary non-WhatsApp accents, highlights, route cues |
| Background | Pale Mist | `#F3F1EA` | Main warm-neutral background |
| Conversion | WhatsApp Green | `#25D366` | WhatsApp action only; do not turn the whole brand green |
| Utility | White | `#FFFFFF` | Reversed logo and clean content surfaces |
| Utility | Near Black | `#161B1F` | Body copy when Midnight Navy lacks sufficient density |

Use solid colors rather than gradients. Maintain WCAG AA contrast.

## Typography Direction

- Display and wordmark direction: Sora SemiBold/Bold.
- Body, UI, captions, and forms: Inter Regular/Medium/SemiBold.
- Use tabular numerals for prices, dates, durations, and comparison data.
- Keep letter spacing at normal values. Do not create artificial luxury with wide tracking everywhere.

Both families are working recommendations. Confirm font licensing and delivery during implementation.

## Production Logo Requirements

Create and test:

- Primary horizontal mark.
- Stacked mark.
- HKS compass icon.
- One-color Midnight Navy mark.
- One-color white/reversed mark.
- Small favicon and social avatar with simplified geometry.
- Vehicle-door version legible at distance.
- Print-safe CMYK specification.

Refinement requirements:

- Make `HKS` legible without requiring explanation.
- Simplify compass points so the mark survives at 16-32 px.
- Keep eastward Saffron movement as an optional accent, not a mandatory tiny detail.
- Correct optical spacing between mark and wordmark.
- Avoid gradients and raster-only masters.
- Remove any appearance of a phone-app icon from presentations and usage guidance.

## Photography

Photography should show the actual promise:

- Real destinations and recognizable landscapes.
- Safari vehicles and viewing conditions.
- Accommodation rooms and shared spaces.
- Meals and included activities.
- Kenyan couples, families, friends, and group organizers where permission exists.
- Guides, trip moments, and practical proof.

Avoid dark stock imagery, fake luxury setups, generic wildlife collages, excessive sunset filters, or images that hide the product.

Only use photographs whose rights the client has confirmed.

## Interface Character

- Attainable-premium, restorative, assured, and vivid.
- Rich destination imagery balanced by very clear logistics.
- Strong hierarchy and generous but efficient spacing.
- Cards only for repeated tours, reviews, or genuine tools. Do not put every section in a floating card.
- WhatsApp green is reserved for the conversion action.
- Use Wayfinder route and compass details sparingly as navigational motifs.
