Day by day

Three days that feel much further from routine.

Day 1

Nairobi to Maasai Mara

Depart Nairobi in the morning and drive through the Great Rift Valley. Arrive at the lodge or camp for lunch, settle in, then head out for an afternoon game drive before dinner.

Day 2

Full day in the reserve

Spend the day tracking wildlife across the Mara. The exact rhythm depends on the guide, season, and lodge location, with time for photography, quiet views, and a proper safari pause.

Day 3

Morning drive and return to Nairobi

Enjoy early tea or coffee, check out, and return to Nairobi with a final chance to take in the landscape. Drop-off is arranged according to the confirmed itinerary.
